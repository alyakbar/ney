Who We Are

A youth-led movement committed to transforming lives through education, mentorship, and community.

About NEYD

North Eastern Youth Development (NEYD) is a grassroots organization founded by passionate young people from North Eastern Kenya.

We believe that with the right guidance, every young person can become a leader, change-maker, and contributor to their community. Our programs connect high school and university students with mentors, resources, and opportunities.

Our Mission

To empower youth through mentorship, education, and leadership development, creating pathways for academic and personal success.

Our Vision

A generation of proactive, empowered young leaders who drive sustainable development and positive change in their communities.

Our Approach

We take a peer-to-peer mentorship approach. University students mentor high schoolers, and professionals guide university students.

This creates a chain of support where each generation lifts the next. Our model is student-led, meaning young people design and drive our programs.
