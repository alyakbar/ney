Collaboration for a greater impact. Together, we go further.

Why Partner With NEYD?

Partnering with NEYD means joining a movement that's transforming the future of youth in North Eastern Kenya.

Your support helps us expand mentorship programs, reach more schools, train more leaders, and create lasting change in underserved communities.

We offer visibility, impact reporting, and meaningful engagement with your organization's CSR goals.
