University Chapters

Student-led groups driving mentorship and community impact on campuses.

What Is a University Chapter?

NEYD University Chapters are student-led groups based on campuses. They serve as the backbone of our mentorship and outreach programs.

Chapter members organize school visits, peer mentorship sessions, career talks, and community service initiatives. They are the bridge between high school students and the academic world.

Who It's For

University Students

  • Leadership experience
  • Volunteering opportunities
  • Career exposure & networking
  • Community impact

High School Students

  • Motivation & inspiration
  • Career guidance
  • University application support
  • Role models from their community
