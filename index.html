Why NEYD Exists

North Eastern Kenya faces unique challenges — limited access to quality education, few mentorship opportunities, and a need for visible role models who understand the region.

NEYD was founded to bridge these gaps. By connecting high school students with university mentors, and university students with professionals, we create a chain of support that uplifts entire communities.
